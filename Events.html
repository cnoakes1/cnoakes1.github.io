Upcoming Community Events

SATURDAY, DECEMBER 7TH, 2019

BREAKFAST WITH SANTA

Are you ho-ho-hungry? Have you always wanted to chow down with the big man himself? Well, grab your kids and come to breakfast with Santa on Saturday, December 7th! The REAL Santa will be there, along with Mrs.Claus and Rudolph, of course (none of the other reindeer though).

LOCATION: The Royal Canadian Legion

ADDRESS: 12 York St N, Lindsay

RUN TIME: 10AM- 1PM

SUNDAY, DECEMBER 16TH, 2019

ANNUAL CHRISTMAS TREE LIGHTING

It’s back again! The annual lighting ceremony for Lindsay’s Christmas tree will be held on Sunday, December 16th, 2019. Don’t forget to bring hot cocoa! Santa will be attending the event and will be available for photos ($10 each).

LOCATION: Kawartha Lakes Public Library

ADDRESS: 190 Kent St W, Lindsay

RUN TIME: 4PM- 7PM

MONDAY, DECEMBER 23RD

MAKE YOUR OWN CHRISTMAS DECORATIONS

Don’t know what to get for mom for Christmas? Make her a gift from the heart! Stop by to create your very own Christmas ornament, keychain, or anything in between, just in time for the holidays!

LOCATION: Lindsay Recreation Complex

ADDRESS: 133 Adelaide St S, Lindsay

RUN TIME: 10AM- 5PM
